About

The Department of Civil Engineering came into inception in the year 1984 providing Diploma in Civil Engineering. In the year 1999, the department also received the laurel of ISO-9004 certification along with the other departments of the institution. In 2003 the existing ISO systems were upgraded to new standards of ISO 9001:2000. In addition to above, the department got the NBA certification from the National Board of Accreditation under All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). Department also spear headed the process to get ISO 14000 accreditation for the institution. The department started B.Tech program in Civil Engineering with effect from 2009. The intake capacity in the Diploma programme is for 60 students. B.Tech has an intake of 30 students in the first year and with additional 30% seat in the second year for lateral entry of diploma students. The department has seven main well equipped labs viz Material Testing Lab, Survey Lab, Geotechnical Lab, Environmental Engineering Lab, Civil Computation Facility, Transportation Engineering Lab and Geology Lab. The department has been responsible for starting a green initiative to maintain green and clean campus by adopting environmental friendly practices such as: Effluent Treatment Plant to treat the night soil and sullage from quarters, hostels and academic buildings within the campus. Paper Recycling Plant to recycle the paper waste generated in the campus. Windrow Composting to convert the organic waste into useful manure which is used in the campus garden. Design and construction of mini gravity dam (BUND) across a perennial stream to recharge the ground water table and to augment the water supply in the campus Converting the existing academic block to a green building in association with IGBC.

Vision

To be a department of excellence to bring out technically competent and socially responsible professionals in the field of Civil Engineering.

Mission

  • To impart quality technical education in the field of Civil Engineering.
  • To develop overall personality of student by imbibing the sense of responsibility towards society and environment, positive attitude & effective communication skill.
  • To facilitate the students to acquire skill for solving technical issues in construction industry through a structured monitoring mechanism of teaching learning process and industrial training.
  • To facilitate development of research aptitude by encouraging students to take up real time / innovative projects.

Lab info

  • CISCO / Networking Laboratory :

    This laboratory provides computing facility for the students of the department of Computer Engineering and Information Technology. It consists of more than 18 high end PCs running Microsoft Windows XP as well as Linux (Red Hat) with dual boot option on top of Intel Pentium IV processor & matching supporting hardware & peripherals and all wired together by the laboratory 100 mbps LAN. To top it, there are two dual processors Windows server running Windows 2000, with the latest add-ons, an Intel Xeona a generic Microsoft Windows NT version 4 servers, one on which is an NT terminal server powering the laboratory. Besides these, there is a local mirror server for Win NT and Win 2000 server to provide the most recent updates to the DBRAIT Port Blair community. Above all, future plan is to set up a high and server running Sun Solaris to be able for high end computational needs of the student's faculty of DBRAIT, Port Blair.

  • Computer Hardware Laboratory :

    The department computer hardware laboratory is equipped with educational tools to promote understanding of computer hardware and its peripherals among the students. 8085/86 Microprocessor Trainer kits and 8033 Microcontroller kits are used to provide hand on experience to learning students about basic hardware. These are ably supported by Colour Logic Analyzer and Function / Arbitrary wave generator, digital oscilloscopes, wireless transmitter /receiver pairs, Data Acquisition/Switch units, TDM pulse code modulator / transmitter and demodulator / receiver and other similar essentials. Students working on various projects have developed microcontroller controlled arms and robots and other similar hardware items.

  • Advanced Computer Laboratory :

    The department provides high end Pentium IV desktops to each of the final year DCE and IT students for their final year dissertation. The department allows the students to configure and maintain their PCs according to their wish and their needs. Additional support and peripherals are provided based on their requirement in the particular projects.

  • Basic Computer Laboratory :

    The regular DCE and IT students are provided with high end desktops machines which is amply supported by additional peripherals an hardware. Every student is made responsible for his / her equipment which is used for the project.
